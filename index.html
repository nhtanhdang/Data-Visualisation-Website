Introduction

This study embarks on a comprehensive analysis of the correlation between food supply metrics and adult obesity rates across 42 countries, spanning the years from 2010 to 2020. Our motivation lies in addressing the pressing global issue of obesity, a multifaceted challenge with significant implications for public health and well-being.

The primary audience for the proposed visualisations includes policymakers, public health officials, researchers, and advocacy groups involved in addressing obesity and dietary-related issues on a global scale. These stakeholders require clear, intuitive, and informative visual representations of data to inform their decision-making processes, prioritise interventions, allocate resources effectively, and track progress over time.


Welcome to Choropleth Map


This interactive map provides a visual representation of various data types across different countries over the years. Explore trends to gain insights into global nutritional habits and health metrics.

  1. Select a Continent: Use the continent buttons to focus on a specific region or view the global data by selecting "All".
  2. Choose a Year: Use the year buttons to view the data for a particular year. The available years range from 2010 to 2020.
  3. Pick a Data Type: Use the data type buttons to visualise different metrics such as Fat intake, Protein consumption, etc.
  4. Interact with the Map: Hover over a country on the map to see its name, the selected year, and the corresponding data value for the chosen metric.
  5. Update Display: Click the "Update" button to apply your selections and refresh the map visualisation.

Welcome to Scatter Plot


Illustrates the dynamic shifts in the supply of key food categories across multiple countries over a decade. The size of each data point corresponds to the obesity rate recorded in that country, offering a nuanced view of the potential relationships between dietary trends and public health outcomes.

  1. Select a Region: Use the dropdown menu to choose a region to focus your analysis.
  2. Choose a Country: Based on the selected region, a list of countries will be populated. Select the country you want to analyse.
  3. Pick a Determinant: Use the dropdown menu to choose the food category you want to visualize on the scatter plot.
  4. Interact with the Scatter Plot: Hover over a data point to see detailed information.
  5. Update Display: Click the "Update" button to apply your selections and refresh the map visualisation.







Welcome to Sankey Chart


Represents the flow of data, specifically showcasing the distribution of food consumption across different food groups and countries within the chosen region, providing an intuitive way to understand the relative proportions and relationships between these elements.

  1. Select a Region: Use the dropdown menu to choose a region to analyse.
  2. Explore the Sankey Chart: The width of the flows represents the relative proportion of consumption for each category.
  3. Analyse Country-Level Data: Select country to narrow down analysis viewpoint.
  4. Hover Over Sankey Links: Hovering over a link (flow) in the Sankey chart and see detailed data value represented by that link.
  5. Update Display: Click the "Update" button to apply your selections and refresh the map visualisation.
